Eating guidelines

Eat within 1 hour of waking — don't skip breakfast before trading or gym.
Prioritize protein at every meal (chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, fish, beef).
Complex carbs around training (oats, rice, potatoes) — reduce carbs at dinner if no gym.
No junk food after 8:00 PM — late-night eating hurts sleep quality and fat loss.
Drink at least 2.5–3L of water daily. Start the morning with a full glass.
Prep meals when possible — don't leave eating to chance on busy days.
